# Whitepaper

This folder contains the technical whitepaper developed as part of the AI Governance and Cybersecurity Risk project.

## Title
Operational Framework for Mitigating Algorithmic Bias in Automated Credit Risk Systems

## Overview
This whitepaper presents a structured methodology to identify, assess, and mitigate algorithmic bias in AI-driven credit decision systems.

The framework translates the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) into a practical and operational model that can be implemented by financial institutions.

## Key Components
- Risk identification in AI-driven credit systems  
- Mapping to NIST AI RMF (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage)  
- Risk assessment model (likelihood, impact, detectability)  
- Design of mitigation controls  
- Continuous monitoring and compliance strategies  

## Objective
The objective of this work is to support secure, fair, and compliant deployment of AI systems in financial services, particularly in automated credit decision-making.

## Relevance
This work aligns with key priorities in the United States related to:
- Artificial intelligence governance  
- Cybersecurity risk management  
- Fair lending and regulatory compliance
