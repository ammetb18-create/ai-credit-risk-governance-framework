## 1. Purpose

This document provides a structured audit checklist for evaluating governance, compliance, risk controls, and regulatory alignment of AI systems used in credit decisioning.

It aligns with:

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)
- SR 11-7 Model Risk Management Guidance
- ECOA / Fair Lending Requirements
- FCRA & Consumer Protection Standards
- Enterprise AI Governance Policies

This checklist supports:

- Internal audit reviews  
- Regulatory examinations  
- Model validation cycles  
- Board-level risk reporting
